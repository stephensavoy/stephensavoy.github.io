Welcome to the Church of Spongebob!

The Church of Spongebob is a joke that should not be taken seriously ever. Most of the content on the website are references to the television show. I hope that this doesn't offend anyone in anyway, but I needed something to make this assignment fun so I don't break my keyboard after getting code wrong for an hour. For even more information please see History.

The Church of Spongebob offers great benefits such as:

· A Church you can enjoy in your own home - no special building to go to.

· A Church that presents it's precepts and spiritual instruction in an easily accessible format.

· A Church that doesn't want your money and only wants you to be happy.

· A Church that realizes that simple things, like having fun, using your imagination, and enjoying what you do, are important in life.

· A Chruch that is totally not a cult.

So ask yourself are you ready to sign up? All it takes to join is a donation of at least $20 to the church
